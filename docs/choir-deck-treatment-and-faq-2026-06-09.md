## Rendering Principles

- Open with product proof, not claims.
- Show the different object: chat answer versus cited, versioned, editable,
  publishable VText.
- Use the product's own design language: serious, calm, serif-forward, generous
  negative space, minimal accent color.
- Avoid generic AI-workspace, crypto, cloud-stock, and SaaS-dashboard cues.
- Keep the deep theory in speaker notes and appendix unless it is needed for
  action.

## FAQ Spine

### Isn't this just another chatbot or AI workspace?

No. Choir is a persistent computer for AI work. Chat can route intent, but the
`AI workspace` phrasing is used here only as a contrast class, not as Choir's
root ontology. The work lives in durable VTexts, source artifacts, agent
notes, candidate computers, publications, and promotion records.

### Why not use GPT or Claude?

Use them. Choir is not competing with model intelligence directly. Choir is the
owned layer where your data, artifacts, routing, evaluations, versions, and
learning compound.

### What do you sell first?

Private Choir Clouds for legal and professional work: client-controlled
infrastructure, source-backed memos, matter memory, client deliverables,
publication controls, and reusable workflow modules.

### What is Wire?

Wire is the source-to-VText substrate. It ingests sources, lets processors,
reconcilers, researchers, and VText agents synthesize them, and produces
source-backed VTexts and editions.

### Is Wire only public news?

No. Public news is the Community Cloud instance. Private Clouds run the same
substrate over private data, subscribed public sources, and domain-specific
source systems.

### Where does personalization happen?

In user computers. User-level processors and reconcilers work over accessible
public/private corpora and ask user VText agents to create personalized
editions, briefings, forks, alerts, and notes.

### How is neutrality handled?

Wire does not act as an oracle. It ingests plurality, preserves provenance, and
surfaces concurrence, contradiction, uncertainty, and emergent questions.
Source behavior and citation consequence can be learned over time without
hardcoding truth tiers.

### Why does source breadth matter?

Human bandwidth forced older systems to privilege a narrow set of authorities.
Large-context models make long-tail, multilingual, social, regional, and
industry sources legible. Filtering them away loses signal.

### What is VText?

VText is versioned semantic state. It is article, memo, report, edition,
styleguide, and publication substrate. Sources and related VTexts are
transcluded, not pasted into metadata lists.

### What is Radio?

Radio is voice/screenless traversal and supervision over the same system. Voice
models are endpoints; the intelligence lives in the owned computers, VTexts,
sources, and agents.

### What is still being figured out?

The contribution/identity model, public social dynamics, private/public egress
review, and the feel of live voice interruption are active design frontiers.
