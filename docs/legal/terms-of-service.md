## 1. Service Description

Choir is a news synthesis platform that:

1. **Fetches content from public sources** — including RSS feeds, Telegram
   channels, GDELT, Polymarket, and public web pages — on a recurring basis.
2. **Deduplicates and synthesizes** that content using large language model
   (LLM) providers to produce original articles that combine and contextualize
   information from multiple sources.
3. **Publishes synthesized articles** on choir.news.
4. **Provides a personal computing surface** where authenticated users can
   create, edit, and version documents ("textures"), run agent-based
   workflows, and interact with an AI-powered prompt bar.

Choir synthesizes content from public sources. The synthesized articles are
original works generated by the system; they are not copies of source content.
Source content remains the property of its original publishers (see Section 5).
